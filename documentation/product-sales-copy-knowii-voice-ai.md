## FAQs

**Q: Is this actually private?**
A: Yes. Everything runs on your computer. Voice recordings stored locally. AI model runs locally. Nothing sent to any server. Run with internet disabled - works perfectly. (Optional cloud models coming, but default is always local-first)

**Q: Does it work offline?**
A: Yes. After one-time AI model download (2-10 GB), everything runs on your computer. Laptop in airplane mode? Still works perfectly.

**Q: How fast is it?**
A: Depends on computer, model choice, and speech length. Decent laptop with small model: 1-3 seconds for few sentences. With GPU acceleration: near-instant. Larger models take few seconds longer but more accurate.

**Q: How accurate is it?**
A: Very. Whisper and Parakeet are among best free speech recognition models available. Accuracy depends on audio quality, background noise, and model. Larger models more accurate but slower. In practice: accurate enough for daily use without constant corrections.

**Q: What languages are supported?**
A: All major languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, and more. Select language in settings. Different models support different languages. Some have specific language variants.

**Q: Will this work on my computer?**
A: Windows 10/11: yes. macOS and Linux: coming soon. Don't need powerful computer, but GPU acceleration helps (NVIDIA CUDA). Works fine on CPU-only, just bit slower.

**Q: What microphone do I need?**
A: Whatever you already have. Built-in laptop mics work. USB mics work. Headset mics work. Better microphones = better results, but don't need anything special.

**Q: Can I customize the keyboard shortcut?**
A: Yes. Default is Ctrl+Space, change to whatever you want in settings.

**Q: What about my clipboard?**
A: App saves clipboard before pasting, restores after. Your copied content isn't lost.

**Q: Can I use this at work?**
A: Yes. Commercial license included. Use for personal projects, professional work, client communications, whatever you need.

**Q: What if I have problems?**
A: Email creator directly. Personal help. If broken, will fix. If confusing, will explain. You're not alone.

**Q: What's the catch with Early Access?**
A: No catch. Product works now. Called Early Access because actively building new features based on user feedback. You help shape what gets built next, and get lowest price ever.

**Q: Will the price increase?**
A: Yes. This is Early Access pricing. When product leaves beta, price goes up. Buy now to lock in lifetime access at this price. No future charges, ever.

**Q: What's your refund policy?**
A: 30-day money-back guarantee. Don't like it? Email for refund. No argument, no hassle.

**Q: How does this relate to your other products?**
A: Part of growing Knowii ecosystem. Voice AI helps capture thoughts faster. Knowii Community helps organize/leverage that knowledge. Knowledge Worker Kit gives frameworks to use it all effectively. Interconnected systems that amplify how you think and work.
